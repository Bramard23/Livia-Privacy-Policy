Introduction

LIVIA ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension ("Extension", "Service") collects, uses, and protects your information.

**By installing and using LIVIA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

**We DO NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) that you provide.**

The Extension does not require you to:
- Create an account
- Provide your name, email, or contact information
- Enter passwords or credentials
- Share personal documents or files

2. Information Automatically Collected

2.1 URL Information (Local Processing Only)

**What we collect:**
- URLs of websites you visit (only when the Extension is active)

**How we use it:**
- To check visited URLs against our local threat database
- To determine if a website is potentially dangerous

**Important clarifications:**
-  URLs are processed **locally in your browser**
-  URLs are **NOT transmitted to our servers** (when using local mode)
-  URLs are **NOT stored permanently**
-  URLs are **NOT shared with third parties**

- ⚠️ If you enable "Enhanced Protection" (optional PRO feature), URLs may be sent to external threat intelligence APIs (VirusTotal, AbuseIPDB) for verification. This is **opt-in only** and clearly disclosed.

2.2 Extension Usage Data

**What we collect:**
- Number of threats blocked
- Number of URLs checked
- Extension settings and preferences

**How we store it:**
- Stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Automatically deleted when you uninstall the Extension

**Purpose:**
- Display statistics to you (e.g., "You've blocked 50 threats this week")
- Remember your preferences
- Improve Extension functionality

2.3 Technical Information

**What we automatically collect:**
- Extension version number
- Browser type and version (e.g., Chrome 120)
- Operating system (e.g., Windows 10, macOS)

**How we use it:**
- Ensure compatibility
- Debug errors
- Provide appropriate updates

**This data is:**
- Anonymous and aggregated
- Cannot be linked to you personally
- Used solely for technical support

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information **only** for the following purposes:

### Primary Purpose: Security Protection

- **Threat Detection:** Check URLs against our database of known phishing sites, malware distributors, and fraudulent websites
- **Real-time Alerts:** Warn you when you attempt to visit a dangerous website
- **Blocking Malicious Sites:** Prevent navigation to high-risk websites (with your permission)

### Secondary Purposes:

- **Display Statistics:** Show you how many threats were blocked and URLs checked
- **Remember Preferences:** Store your settings (e.g., notification preferences, blocking level)
- **Improve Service:** Aggregate anonymous usage data to improve threat detection algorithms
- **Provide Support:** Diagnose technical issues if you contact us for help

### We DO NOT:

❌ Sell your data to third parties  
❌ Use your data for advertising or marketing  
❌ Track your browsing behavior for profiling  
❌ Share your data with data brokers  
❌ Use your data for purposes unrelated to security  


Data Storage and Retention

### Local Storage (Browser)

**Where:** All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API

**What:** 
- Threat database (list of known malicious URLs)
- Your settings and preferences
- Usage statistics

**Retention:**
- Data persists until you uninstall the Extension
- You can clear data at any time via Extension settings
- Data is automatically deleted upon uninstallation

### Server Storage (Optional - PRO Feature Only)

If you upgrade to LIVIA PRO and enable "Enhanced Protection":

**What we may store:**
- Hashed URLs (one-way encrypted, cannot be reversed to original URL)
- Threat detection results
- API request logs (for rate limiting)

**Where:** Secure servers in the European Union (GDPR compliant)

**Retention:**
- Hashed URLs: 24 hours
- Detection results: 7 days (for cache purposes)
- Logs: 30 days (for security audits)

**Deletion:** 
- Automatically deleted after retention period
- You can request immediate deletion by contacting us

Specific Permission Justifications

Our Extension requests the following permissions. Here's what we use them for:

### storage
**Purpose:** Save your settings and preferences locally in your browser  
**Data:** Extension settings, threat statistics, user preferences  
**Shared:** No, stays on your device

### tabs
**Purpose:** Access the URL of the current tab to check if it's safe  
**Data:** Current tab URL only (not content, not history)  
**Shared:** No, processed locally

### webNavigation
**Purpose:** Intercept navigation to potentially dangerous websites and block them  
**Data:** URLs you're navigating to  
**Shared:** No, processed locally (unless Enhanced Protection enabled)

### notifications
**Purpose:** Alert you when a threat is detected  
**Data:** No data collection, only notification display  
**Shared:** No

### host_permissions: <all_urls>
**Purpose:** Check any website you visit for threats (we can't predict which sites will be malicious)  
**Data:** URLs visited  
**Shared:** No, processed locally by default
